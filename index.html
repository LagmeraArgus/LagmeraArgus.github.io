Lagmera Argus

Thotting it up in Eorzea

Bio

Lagmera Argus is a woman of many contradictions. She dresses in expensive finery, yet has the mouth of a sailor. She is a tall, statuesque Viera beauty, yet behaves in a brash and impetuous manner. She is fabulously wealthy, but never works, nor manages her money. She flirts with nearly every attractive stranger that crosses her path, yet is never affianced to any of them. She surrounds herself with classy and elegant luxuries, yet never wears an outfit that is not shamefully revealing. She has a quick mind and quicker wit, yet is impressively ignorant of many topics, revealing a lack of any kind of formal education.

Many would describe her in negative terms: vapid, disreputable, coquettish, crass, rude, and completely lacking self-control. At the same time, however, she manages to charm those around her, bringing smiles and laughter with her presence. Despite her coarse language, she does her best to delight, not insult those she banters with, and many find her surprisingly-empathetic. For better or worse, everything she feels, she feels to an extreme degree, and can never hide it.
